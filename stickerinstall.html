Pinkel Stickers on iPhone or Android

Follow the short 10 step proccess below to install the Pinkel Stickers on iPhone or Android devices!

  • Install Pinkel Stickers Now

Step 1

Click on the "Install Pinkel Stickers Now" button above.

Step 2

You will see the Pinkel Stickers page. Install the Stickery app by clicking one of the two buttons at the bottom show above. (iPhone or Android)

Step 3

Once installed, go back to the Pinkel Sticker page (repeat Step 1) and click the big blue "GET PACK" button, then click "Open".

Step 4

You will be directed into the Stickery app, and you will see the image shown above. Click the blue "Import pack" button at the bottom.

Step 5

Once the Pinkel Stickers are imported, select the green "CREATE PACK" button.

Step 6

After it's been created, press the blue "EXPORT" button at the bottom.

Step 7

The "EXPORT" button will slide up. Select the blue "Messages" button.

Step 8

When you click "EXPORT", an advertisement may pop up. X out the advertisement, and wait untill you recieve the "Ready!" message.

Step 9

Once the Pinkel Stickers are ready, you can now send them in text messages!
For iPhone users, go to a text message, click the App icon next to the Camera and find the Stickery App icon (as shown above), and the Pinkel Stickers will open up.

Step 10

To put stickers on messages, hold down a sticker you want to use, and drag it to your message. Once your message turns dark blue, you can let go of the sticker! You can also resize and rotate the sticker as well.
