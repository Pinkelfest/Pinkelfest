Pinkel Stickers Guide

To put stickers on messages, send a text message, then hold down a sticker you want to use, and drag it to your message. Once your message turns dark blue, you can let go of the sticker! You can also resize and rotate the sticker as well.
Watch video above to see how it works. (Viewable on mobile only at this time)

Read steps or watch video above first before installing!

Step 1

Click on the "Install Pinkel Stickers Now" button above.

Step 2

You will see the Pinkel Stickers page. Install the Stickery app by clicking one of the two buttons at the bottom show above (iPhone or Android), then click "Open".

Step 3

Once installed, go back to the Pinkel Sticker page (repeat Step 1) and click the big blue "GET PACK" button, then click "Open".

Step 4

You will be directed into the Stickery app, and you will see the image shown above. Click the blue "Import pack" button at the bottom.

An advertisment may pop up. X out the advertisement.
(An X button will be on top left or right of ad)

Step 5

After it's been imported, click the Pinkel Stickers icon, then click the "EXPORT" button at the bottom, and select the blue "Messages" button.
An advertisment may pop up. X out the advertisement
(An X button will be on top left or right of ad)
and wait for the "Ready!" message.

Step 6

Once the Pinkel Stickers are ready, you can now send them in text messages!
Go to a text message, click the App icon next to the Camera and find the Stickery App icon (as shown above), and the Pinkel Stickers will open up.

Step 7

To put stickers on messages, send a text message, then hold down a sticker you want to use, and drag it to your message. Once your message turns dark blue, you can let go of the sticker! You can also resize and rotate the sticker as well.
Watch video above to see how it works. (Viewable on mobile only at this time)
